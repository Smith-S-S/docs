🧠 1️⃣ What is AI Governance? (Plain English)

👉 AI Governance = Rules + Processes to control how AI is built and used safely

Think:

“How do we make sure AI doesn’t break things, harm users, or create risk?”

🔧 Example (Non-technical)

If a company uses AI:

Who approves the model?
How is it tested?
What data is allowed?
What happens if it fails?

👉 That entire control system = AI Governance

🛡️ 2️⃣ What is Responsible AI?

👉 Responsible AI = Making AI ethical, fair, safe, and trustworthy

Focus on:

fairness
transparency
safety
accountability
🔧 Example

If an AI system:

gives biased answers
leaks sensitive data
generates harmful output

👉 That means it is NOT responsible AI

⚠️ 3️⃣ What is AI Risk Management?

👉 Identifying + reducing risks from AI systems

Types of Risks
🔐 Data privacy risk
🎯 wrong predictions
⚖️ bias / unfair decisions
🧠 hallucination (LLMs)
🔓 security issues
🔗 4️⃣ How These Three Connect

Think like this:

Responsible AI → what “good AI” looks like
AI Governance → how you enforce it
AI Risk Management → how you control risks
